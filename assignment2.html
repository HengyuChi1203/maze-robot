Reflection

During the development of our maze-mapping robot project, we encountered significant challenges, ranging from algorithmic design flaws to unexpected hardware-software integration issues. Debugging pathfinding logic and ensuring sensor accuracy proved particularly demanding, often requiring iterative testing and collaboration. However, the team demonstrated remarkable resilience and dedication. Through shared research, peer mentoring, and persistent experimentation, we gradually overcame obstacles while deepening our understanding of robotics principles. This experience highlighted the value of adaptability and teamwork in problem-solving. Though the process was arduous, it ultimately reinforced our confidence in tackling complex technical tasks through continuous learning and collective effort.
